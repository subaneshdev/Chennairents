Where 2BHK rents are moving fastest

By the chennairents.in Editorial Team

The 2BHK (two-bedroom apartment) is the most sought-after configurations in Chennai. Offering the ideal balance of space and budget for small families, roommates, and young professionals, 2BHK rental rates serve as a key market health indicator. We analyze the suburbs experiencing the fastest 2BHK rent movements.

1. Velachery: The Commuter Hotspot

Velachery continues to lead the city in 2BHK transaction volume. With high demand from IT employees wanting access to OMR while maintaining central city connectivity, average 2BHK rates in Velachery have moved from ₹21,000 to ₹25,000 over the past year. High-end standalone structures and low-rise apartments are driving this growth.

2. Medavakkam: The Suburban Alternative

For tenants priced out of Velachery, Medavakkam has become the primary destination. Connected directly via the Velachery-Tambaram main road, Medavakkam's 2BHK rents have moved up by 11% to land at an average of ₹16,000 to ₹19,000. It offers modern residential layouts at a significant discount compared to the city center.

3. Sholinganallur: The IT Anchor

At the center of OMR, Sholinganallur's 2BHK market has seen significant upward pressure. Gated community 2BHKs in Sholinganallur command ₹24,000 to ₹30,000 depending on furnishing status, representing a 10.5% yearly increase. Proximity to major SEZs keeps demand consistently high.
