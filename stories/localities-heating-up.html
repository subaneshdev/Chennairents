Which Chennai localities are heating up?

By the chennairents.in Editorial Team

Analyzing tenant-reported rent data reveals specific sub-markets in Chennai that are experiencing major demand surges. Using our crowdsourced map registry, we identified the top heating rental corridors across the city in 2026.

1. The OMR IT Corridor (Sholinganallur & Perungudi)

Unsurprisingly, the technology hubs along OMR continue to see robust rental demand. With corporate mandates pushing employees back to hybrid and full office schedules, Sholinganallur and Perungudi have seen rent inflation of 12-14% over the last year. Gated societies are especially high in demand, with listings closing within 48 hours of posting.

2. The West Chennai Expansion (Porur)

Porur has emerged as a major growth sector. Driven by the expansion of the DLF IT Park and nearby medical colleges, rental demand for 2BHKs has surged. Porur offers a more accessible starting price point than OMR but has seen a rapid 10% rent price growth over the past 12 months.

3. Transit-Oriented Hotspots (Velachery & Guindy)

Proximity to transit remains a major driver of rental value. Velachery and Guindy, linked by the MRTS and metro lines, are heating up significantly. Rents for apartments located within 1km of these transit stations command a premium that is growing faster than outlying residential lanes.
