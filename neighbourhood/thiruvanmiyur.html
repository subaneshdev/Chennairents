Why People Choose Thiruvanmiyur

MRTS Connectivity: Thiruvanmiyur station is on the MRTS Beach–Velachery line, offering a traffic-free commute to Mandaveli, Kotturpuram, and Chennai central. Peak-hour MRTS beats road traffic by 30–40 minutes.
OMR Access: The Rajiv Gandhi Salai corridor starts effectively from Thiruvanmiyur. Sholinganallur, Perungudi, and Tidel Park are all within a 10–15 minute drive on most days — significantly shorter than commuting from deeper OMR localities.
Coastal Living: Thiruvanmiyur has direct access to Thiruvanmiyur Beach and is minutes from Besant Nagar Beach (Elliot's Beach). This lifestyle premium is rare at this price point — comparable coastal areas in Adyar command 25–30% higher rent.
Water Supply: Thiruvanmiyur generally has good Metrowater coverage compared to deeper OMR localities. However, during summer, some streets still rely on tanker supplements. Always confirm the water source before finalising a flat.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the average rent in Thiruvanmiyur Chennai?
A: A 2BHK flat in Thiruvanmiyur typically costs ₹22,000–₹30,000 per month. 1BHK units range from ₹13,000 to ₹18,000. Gated complexes with full amenities can go up to ₹35,000+ for a 2BHK.
Q: Is Thiruvanmiyur good for IT professionals?
A: Yes. Thiruvanmiyur is one of the best-located IT professional zones in South Chennai — MRTS rail to central Chennai, and direct ECR/Rajiv Gandhi Salai access to Tidel Park, Sholinganallur, and Perungudi IT hubs. The coastal quality of life is an added bonus.
Q: How is MRTS from Thiruvanmiyur?
A: Very convenient. Thiruvanmiyur MRTS station is on the Beach–Velachery line. Trains are regular during peak hours. The MRTS bypasses the road congestion on ECR and Lattice Bridge Road, making it the fastest way to reach central Chennai from Thiruvanmiyur.
Q: What is the typical advance deposit in Thiruvanmiyur?
A: Landlords typically ask for 6–8 months' advance. The proximity to premium Adyar keeps deposit norms slightly higher than suburban areas. IT professionals with employment proof can usually negotiate to 5–6 months.
