Frequently Asked Questions about Renting in Chennai

How does chennai.rent work?

Tap anywhere on the map and drop your rent anonymously. Other renters see real prices in their area. If you're flat-hunting, drop a seeker pin with your budget — we email you when matching listings appear within 2.5km.

Is chennai.rent free? Do I need to sign up?

Yes, free for everyone. No signup, no login, no app required. We don't charge tenants or owners and have no plans to. Rents are crowdsourced for local transparency.

How is chennai.rent different from broker sites?

Standard sites show broker-quoted listings, which are often inflated. chennai.rent shows real rents shared by actual current and past tenants, letting you see historical averages to guide negotiation.
